The Supermarket at the Heart of the Yorkshire Dales

Campbell's of Leyburn is an award-winning, family-owned supermarket and world-class wine retailer that has been meeting the needs - and exceeding the expectations - of people who live in, and visit, the Yorkshire Dales since 1868.

In addition to offering its own distinctive style of stress-free supermarket shopping, Campbell's is a visitor-attraction in its own right. Its thriving Wine Club with an in-store, events area, and renowned traditional butchery and Deli are among features that attract visitors from far afield.

While supermarkets from Canterbury to Carlisle tend to look and feel the same, we offer something entirely different. Of course, it helps to be based in a stunningly beautiful corner of England.

  • We support proactively local Yorkshire farmers, artisans and specialist food manufacturers, whilst catering equally diligently for the increasingly cosmopolitan tastes of locals and visitors.
  • We believe we offer the widest selection of wines in the region, plus an outstanding selection of spirits and ales.
  • Our traditional butchers pride themselves on sourcing local and rare breeds' meat.
  • Our delicatessen and cheese counters offer exceptional selections of local, national and international products.
